$$u=x \  v'=ch(x)dx$$
$$u'=dx \ v=sh(x)$$
$$\displaystyle [a]\left(xsh(x) -\int sh(x)dx\right) -\int ch(x)dx =[a]xsh(x) -[a]ch(x)-sh(x)+C$$
